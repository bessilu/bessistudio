About

Sandro Bessi was born in Florence in 1960. He carried out his studies at the Florence Academy of Fine Arts and in 1985 he started his own design studio in Tuscany. Lucia Aristei was born in Florence in 1961, and Since 1985 she started working in her husband studio as head of administration, editorial graphics and customer support. Sandro bessi is member of ADI (Industrial Design Association) and BEDA (Bureau of European Design Association). The studio offers consultancy and industrial design service and boast a twenty years specialization in the crystal, glass and porcelain field, expecially in giftware, tableware and accessories.
